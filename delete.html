Delete My Account & Data

We respect your right to remove your data at any time.

You can request deletion of your TroTro+ account and all associated personal data at any time. We process all deletion requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your phone number and account credentials
  • Ride history and route preferences
  • Payment references and transaction records
  • Location history and app activity data

What gets retained

Some data may be retained where required by law (e.g. financial transaction records for tax/regulatory compliance) for up to 7 years.

How to request deletion

1
Send an email to our support team using the button below.
2
Include the phone number linked to your TroTro+ account.
3
We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.
