Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters — here's exactly what we collect and why.

TroTro+ ("we", "our", or "us") operates a mobile transit platform designed to digitize Ghana's informal trotro minibus system. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

Account Information Your phone number (used for authentication via OTP), preferred language, and user role (rider or driver).
Location Data GPS location when the app is in use — used to show nearby routes, stops, and real-time trotro positions. We do not collect location in the background without your consent.
Payment Information Transaction references and amounts processed via Hubtel or Paystack. We do not store full payment card details — these are handled securely by our payment partners.
Usage Data App interactions, route searches, and ride history — used to improve the service and personalize your experience.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity via SMS OTP authentication
  • To show real-time trotro routes, stops, and arrival estimates
  • To process mobile fare payments securely
  • To send ride confirmations and service notifications
  • To improve app performance and fix issues
  • To comply with applicable Ghanaian laws and regulations

3 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • AfricasTalking — for SMS OTP delivery
  • Hubtel / Paystack — for payment processing
  • Railway.app — our secure cloud infrastructure provider
  • Law enforcement or regulators, only when legally required

4 Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. financial transaction records).

5 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking at any time via device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

6 Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed authentication tokens, and secure cloud infrastructure. No system is 100% secure, but we take your data protection seriously.

7 Children's Privacy

TroTro+ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of TroTro+ after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
